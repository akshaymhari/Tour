Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of kerala, which is known as Gods own country, is highlighted place in the tourist map. This is not only a capital town, but also famous for tourist spot, which never frustrates people who reach their. Thiruvananthapuram is located in the southern part of kerala, and it is a wonderful place with sea shore where people can enjoy the breeze and chillness of mist. Ponmudi(Belongs to Hirange ) is really a beckoning place with it's natural beauty. Thiruvananthapuram is the only such a gorgeous place, where tourist can luxuriate, both the beauty of jungle and beaches. Thiruvananthapuram attracts people, as It combines the gallop of this area, along with its tranquility.
