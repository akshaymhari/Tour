Kochi

Place which is known as the Queen of Arabian Sea. As its name denotes, it is a place with the splendor of a queen, which has marked its spot as a tourist place . Kochi is the paradise of tourists , as it reflects a cross section of real human life . The beauty of the beaches (sea shores ) and it quietness, shopping amenities provided by the vendors, its skyscrapers, and scenic beauty are really amazing factors to attracts tourists there .
